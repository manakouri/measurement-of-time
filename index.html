Vocabulary Reference

Maintain: To keep something going at the same level.

Predict: To say what will happen in the future based on evidence.

Rotation: The action of spinning around a central point or an axis.

Velocity: The speed of something moving in a specific direction.

Constant: Something that does not change.

Interval: The space or time between two points.

Orbit: The curved path of a celestial object around a star, planet, or moon.

Attract: To pull something closer using an invisible force.

Stability: The state of being steady and not changing or failing.
